About Frogger

Frogger is a game that we enjoyed playing on the home Atari games. Back then, it was state of the art. Something that my children would probably laugh at today, and get bored with very quickly. The object of the game was to maneuver your frog through traffic, across the backs of alligators, and more. With each completed level, the traffi and other obstacles moved faster and faster. It's getting cold in here.

Maps Maps and More Maps

One of the greatest things about the web is the ability to find anything on a map. Without a doubt, Google has made the world a smaller place, simply by mapping it. (I doubt it was terribly simple) If you think about it, you literally never have to ask for directions. You can travel anywhere in the U.S with very little effort.
